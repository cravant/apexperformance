COMMON QUESTIONS

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does a typical tune take?

A basic ECU tune takes 2-4 hours on the dyno. More complex builds with custom mapping can take a full day. We'll give you an accurate timeline during consultation.

Do you work on all makes and models?

We specialize in import and domestic performance cars—Japanese, European, and American. If it's got an engine and a performance aftermarket, we can tune it.

What's included in your tuning services?

All tunes include baseline dyno pulls, custom map calibration, AFR optimization, multiple test pulls, and final dyno sheets. You get documentation of every run.

Can I watch my car being tuned?

Absolutely. We encourage it. Watch your car on the dyno, see the numbers climb, and ask as many questions as you want. We love showing our process.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. For larger builds, we offer flexible payment options. We accept major credit cards, and financing is available for qualified customers.

What's your warranty policy?

We warranty all our labor and stand behind our work. Specific warranties vary by service—we'll discuss coverage during your consultation.
