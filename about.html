OUR STORY

BUILT BY ENTHUSIASTS

More than a shop—we're a team of racers, tuners, and fabricators who live and breathe performance.

From Track to Shop

APEX Performance was born in 2012 from a simple truth: if you want something done right, do it yourself. Our founder, Marcus Chen, spent years racing in amateur circuits, wrenching on his own builds, and getting frustrated with tuning shops that talked a big game but delivered mediocre results.

So he built his own shop. Not a quick-lube operation slapping on bolt-on parts, but a real performance facility with a Dynojet, fabrication equipment, and a team of technicians who actually understand motorsport engineering.

Over a decade later, we've built hundreds of cars—from daily-driven street machines to podium-finishing race builds. Every car that leaves our facility carries our reputation, and we take that seriously.
